about us

it takes a village to spin a record.

Silver Village Records is an independent record store located in the heart of Honolulu, proudly serving music lovers since 1998.

We offer a wide selection of both new and used vinyl and CDs. Our catalogue spans a multitude of genres, including rock, pop, jazz, hip-hop,and electronic!

Whether you're searching for some classics, checking out what's new in music, or just want to browse, everyone is welcome in our bins!

Come stop by! We're located at
